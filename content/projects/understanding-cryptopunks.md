Understanding CryptoPunks is a comprehensive guide by jalil.eth that explores everything from a high-level overview of the phenomenon to the actual smart contract code and how the marketplace works. Published in partnership with Visualize Value.

## Chapters

1. **A High Level View** - What in the world are CryptoPunks?
2. **The Art** - A closer look at the visual art of CryptoPunks
3. **Talking Computers** - Code basics for understanding blockchain
4. **Reading the Contract** - A deep dive into the CryptoPunks smart contract
5. **Further Reading** - Wrapped Punks, Onchain Punks, V1 Wrapper

## What You'll Learn

- The history and cultural significance of CryptoPunks
- How the original contract works and why it matters
- The technical details behind punk attributes and rarity
- The ecosystem of wrapped punks and derivatives
