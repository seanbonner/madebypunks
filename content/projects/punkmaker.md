PunkMaker is an interactive character creator that lets you remix and customize punk characters using the original 24x24 pixel art style.

## Features

- **Full Customization** - Adjust every attribute: hair, eyes, mouth, accessories, and more
- **3D Preview** - Real-time 3D rendering of your creation
- **All 87 Attributes** - Access every original CryptoPunks attribute
- **Export** - Download your custom punk as an image

## Customizable Traits

- Type (human, zombie, ape, alien)
- Hairstyle & headwear
- Eyes & eyewear
- Facial hair & mouth
- Skin markings
- Neck accessories
- And more...

Remix all the punks!
