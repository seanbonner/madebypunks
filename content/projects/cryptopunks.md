CryptoPunks is where it all began. Created by Matt Hall and John Watkinson of Larva Labs in June 2017, this marketplace is home to 10,000 unique, algorithmically generated 24x24 pixel portraits inspired by the London punk scene and cyberpunk culture.

## Features

- **Live Marketplace** - Buy, sell, and bid on CryptoPunks with real-time pricing data
- **Transaction History** - Complete sales history and market statistics
- **Attribute Explorer** - Filter punks by their 87 distinct attributes
- **Owner Leaderboards** - See the biggest collectors in the ecosystem
- **Wrapped Punk Support** - Trade ERC721-wrapped punks

## The Collection

- 6,039 male humans
- 3,840 female humans
- 88 zombies
- 24 apes
- 9 aliens

Lifetime sales have exceeded 1.4M ETH ($3.9B+), making CryptoPunks one of the most valuable NFT collections ever created.
