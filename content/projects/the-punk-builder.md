The Punk Builder lets you create and customize your own punk-style characters. Built by The Punk Animator, it provides tools to mix and match attributes to design unique pixel art punks.

## Features

- **Character Creator** - Build punks from scratch
- **Attribute Selection** - Choose from various traits and accessories
- **Preview Mode** - See your creation in real-time
- **Export Options** - Save your custom punk as an image

Created by the same artist behind The Punk Animator animations.
