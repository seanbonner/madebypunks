Punkism reimagines CryptoPunks through various artistic movements and styles. The series includes tributes to Warhol's pop art, Picasso's cubism, Rembrandt's classical portraiture, as well as explorations into meme culture, comic art, and film aesthetics.

Notable works include Rembrandt Punk, Six Marilyns, Crying Wojak Punk, and Surrealism Punk.
